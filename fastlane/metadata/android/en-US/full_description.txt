<p>Miles Client is a mobile companion app for the Miles CRM system, designed for field agents who need real-time location tracking and lead management capabilities.</p>

<p><b>Features:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Real-time GPS tracking with background location service</li>
<li>Push notifications for lead assignments via OneSignal</li>
<li>Secure authentication with JWT tokens</li>
<li>Lead management and status updates</li>
<li>Offline capabilities with automatic sync</li>
<li>Integration with Netlify-hosted CRM backend</li>
</ul>

<p><b>Note:</b> This app requires a connection to the Miles CRM backend service at earnest-jelly-104eb3.netlify.app</p>
